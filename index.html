About Us

A 9-minute walk from the Junction Square shopping mall, this modern hotel features an art nouveau exterior, and is 4 km from Shwedagon Pagoda. Sophisticated rooms and 1-bedroom suites have Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minibars, as well as tea and coffeemakers. All also include desks and sitting areas with sofas. Upgraded rooms offer iPod docks and rainfall showers; suites add separate sitting rooms. There's also 24-hour room service. Breakfast is offered (fee). There are also 3 restaurants; 1 is French with 3 terraces. Other amenities include bars, an outdoor pool, a fitness centre and a spa. Parking is available. In addition to the standard of ALLSAFE, all guests get free Wi-Fi in all rooms and free parking if arriving by car. Conveniently situated in the Kamaryut part of Yangon, this property puts you close to attractions and interesting dining options. Don't leave before paying a visit to the famous Shwedagon Pagoda. Rated with 5 stars, this high-quality property provides guests with access to massage, restaurant and hot tub on-site.
